Machine Learning Overview

Machine Learning (ML) is a subset of artificial intelligence (AI) focused on building systems that can learn from data, identify patterns, and make decisions with minimal human intervention. Rather than being explicitly programmed for specific tasks, ML algorithms enable machines to "learn" from experience and improve their performance over time.

Key topics include Data,Algorithms,Training,Features,Model,Supervised vs. Unsupervised Learning,Reinforcement Learning.
